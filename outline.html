Cyber Security Course Details

The Cyber Security course is designed to provide you with the knowledge and skills to protect information and networks from cyber threats. This course covers a range of essential topics, from basic security principles to advanced techniques for safeguarding your organization's digital assets.

Course Overview

In today's digital world, cyber security is more important than ever. This course equips students with the tools to safeguard information, detect threats, and respond to incidents efficiently. It includes hands-on labs, real-world examples, and a deep dive into security technologies such as firewalls, encryption, and intrusion detection systems (IDS).

Learning Objectives

  • Understand fundamental concepts of Cyber Security, including threat models and risk management.
  • Learn how to protect your network infrastructure from external and internal attacks.
  • Explore techniques for securing applications, systems, and data from security breaches.
  • Gain practical skills in using cyber security tools such as penetration testing, firewalls, and encryption.
  • Learn about ethical hacking and the importance of securing your organization's digital assets.

Course Outline

  • Module 1: Introduction to Cyber Security
    • Cyber Threats and Attacks
    • Cyber Security Basics
    • Cyber Laws and Ethics
  • Module 2: Network Security
    • Firewalls and Network Monitoring
    • Virtual Private Networks (VPNs)
    • Intrusion Detection and Prevention Systems (IDS/IPS)
  • Module 3: Cryptography and Encryption
    • Basic Cryptography Concepts
    • Symmetric vs Asymmetric Encryption
    • Encryption Tools and Techniques
  • Module 4: Ethical Hacking and Penetration Testing
    • Penetration Testing Methods
    • Vulnerability Assessment
    • Tools and Techniques for Ethical Hacking
  • Module 5: Security Management
    • Risk Management and Mitigation
    • Incident Response and Recovery
    • Building a Security Plan for Your Organization

Who Should Take This Course?

This course is ideal for professionals working in IT or network administration, as well as those looking to start a career in Cyber Security. It is suitable for:

  • Network and System Administrators
  • IT Security Analysts
  • Anyone interested in ethical hacking and penetration testing
  • Security professionals looking to upgrade their skills

Prerequisites

  • Basic understanding of computer networks and operating systems (Windows/Linux).
  • Familiarity with common IT terminology.
  • No prior cyber security experience is required, although it may be beneficial.

Course Duration

The Cyber Security course is designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of both the theoretical and practical aspects of securing computer systems and networks. The duration of the course is structured to ensure that each student is given the right amount of time to grasp complex topics while also offering flexibility for those with busy schedules. Below is an overview of the course's schedule and time commitment.

Duration Overview

The course lasts for 12 weeks (3 months). Each week, students will dedicate approximately 6-8 hours to the course, which includes time for attending live sessions, completing assignments, engaging with course materials, and participating in hands-on labs and group discussions.

Weekly Breakdown

  • Live Sessions: 2 hours per week (Every Wednesday at 7:00 PM - 9:00 PM GMT)
  • Self-paced Learning: 4-6 hours per week (Access to pre-recorded videos, readings, and quizzes)
  • Lab Sessions: 1-2 hours per week (Hands-on practical exercises using real-world scenarios)
  • Assignments: Weekly assignments and quizzes (30 minutes to 1 hour per assignment)
  • Discussion Forum: Active participation in the online discussion forum (30 minutes to 1 hour per week)

Course Structure

The course is divided into 3 main phases, each designed to focus on different aspects of Cyber Security:

  • Phase 1: Introduction to Cyber Security (Weeks 1–4)
    • Foundational knowledge in Cyber Security
    • Understanding security principles and frameworks
    • Introduction to network security and ethical hacking
  • Phase 2: Advanced Cyber Security Techniques (Weeks 5–8)
    • Deep dive into penetration testing and vulnerability assessment
    • Learning about cryptography and data protection techniques
    • Real-world applications of security protocols and firewalls
  • Phase 3: Capstone Project & Exam Preparation (Weeks 9–12)
    • Practical penetration testing and live lab exercises
    • Preparing for the final exam and certifications
    • Final project: Conducting a full security audit and penetration test for a simulated company

Flexible Learning Options

We understand that students come from various backgrounds and may have different schedules, so the course offers a blend of synchronous and asynchronous learning. This allows for flexibility in learning while still maintaining the integrity and rigor of the course.

  • Synchronous Learning: Live sessions will be held weekly, allowing students to interact with the instructor in real-time, ask questions, and engage in discussions with peers.
  • Asynchronous Learning: Students can access all recorded sessions, study materials, quizzes, and assignments at their own pace. This flexibility allows students to learn when it fits into their schedule.
  • Weekend Bootcamps: Additional bootcamps are available on weekends for students who prefer more intensive study sessions and hands-on practice. These are optional and can be attended as extra practice.

Time Commitment

To succeed in the course, students are expected to commit to the following:

  • 6–8 hours per week for self-paced learning, including watching videos, completing assignments, and reading course materials.
  • 2 hours per week for the live session, where you can engage with the instructor and your fellow students in real-time.
  • 1–2 hours per week for lab sessions where you’ll apply what you've learned in practical, hands-on scenarios.

Final Exam & Certification

The final exam will be held in the last week of the course (Week 12). It will test your understanding of key concepts covered throughout the course. Students who successfully complete the exam and the capstone project will receive a certification of completion in Cyber Security, which can be shared with employers or added to your professional portfolio.

Course Fee

The Cyber Security course is designed to provide students with high-quality education and practical experience in the field of cyber security. The course fee reflects the comprehensive curriculum, access to expert instructors, hands-on labs, and the support provided throughout the course. Below is a breakdown of the fee structure, payment plans, and additional information.

Course Fee Overview

The full cost of the Cyber Security course is $1,200 USD. This fee includes everything you need to successfully complete the course and gain a professional understanding of cyber security. The following items are included in the course fee:

  • Access to all course materials: Recorded lectures, reading materials, practice quizzes, and weekly assignments.
  • Live sessions: Weekly live instructor-led classes for direct interaction, Q&A, and real-time support.
  • Hands-on labs: Practical experience through labs and simulations, including real-world scenarios and penetration testing exercises.
  • Capstone project: Final project that allows students to apply the skills learned throughout the course in a real-world context.
  • Final exam: Access to a comprehensive final exam to test your knowledge and understanding of the course material.
  • Certification: A digital certificate upon successful completion of the course and final exam. This certificate can be shared with potential employers or added to your professional portfolio.
  • Access to discussion forums: Engage with peers and instructors in the online community for ongoing support and collaboration.

Payment Options

We offer a variety of payment options to ensure the course is accessible to all learners:

  • Full Payment Option: Pay the full course fee of $1,200 USD upfront. This option is ideal for students who prefer to pay once and avoid any recurring payments.
  • Installment Payment Plan: Spread the cost over 3 months with monthly payments of $400 USD. The first payment is due upon enrollment, with subsequent payments due each month until the course is fully paid off.
  • Early Bird Discount: Enroll before [Insert Early Bird Deadline Date] and save 10% on the total course fee. This means the course will cost only $1,080 USD if you sign up early.
  • Group Discount: If you are enrolling with a group (3 or more students), you will receive a 15% discount on the total course fee. The discounted price for each student is $1,020 USD.
  • Corporate/Employer Sponsorship: If your employer is sponsoring your education, we can arrange for direct invoicing and payment. Contact us to get more information on corporate pricing and bulk enrollment discounts.

What’s Not Included in the Fee?

While the course fee covers all core learning materials and access to live sessions, there are a few additional optional costs to be aware of:

  • Optional Textbooks: Although most materials are provided online, students may wish to purchase additional textbooks or reference materials. These are not included in the course fee and are optional.
  • External Certifications: If you decide to pursue additional certifications (such as CompTIA Security+, CISSP, etc.) after completing the course, there may be additional exam fees charged by certifying bodies.
  • Hardware/Software: All students will need a laptop or desktop computer with internet access and the ability to run virtual machines for lab work. Specific software or hardware requirements will be communicated to you upon enrollment, and while most of the software is free or open-source, students may incur small additional costs for certain tools or platforms.

Refund Policy

We understand that life can be unpredictable, so we offer a flexible refund policy to ensure you can enroll in the course with confidence:

  • Full Refund: If you decide to withdraw from the course within the first 7 days after enrollment, you will receive a full refund of your course fee.
  • Partial Refund: If you withdraw after 7 days but before the start of Week 3, you will receive a 50% refund of your total course fee.
  • No Refund: After Week 3, the course fee becomes non-refundable, as you will have accessed a significant portion of the course materials and live sessions.
